New Stewards of Planet Earth

The End is Nigh...

Given our propensity for war and planet harming activities, it is my belief that the human race is doomed and in effect animals will take over the world. Animals are quite diverse and have propensities for surviving dire circumstances. Various animals will inherit unique niches and flourish: apex predators roaming diminished cityscapes and countrysides, the seas booming in our absence with marine life, planet Earth will return to its once feral and primal origins.

Cling to Your Code!!

We have the option of awaiting the inevitable and laying around while Earth continues its certain path towards homicide or we can embrace the future and all man, woman and child can take up computer programming and begin to develop the software for intergalactic travels. You can start here.
