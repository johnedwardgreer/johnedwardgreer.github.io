Welcome to my Website!

5 years ago living and loving San Diego(my home town), I decided I needed a change and some new challenges. I picked up my life, jumped in my Jeep and translated north to San Francisco for grad school @ USF!

Though San Diego will always have a special place in my heart for its ubiquitous beauty, my family still residing there, and let's be honest: the burrito scene - I love the culture and city living in the Bay Area and calling it home!

Currently I am working in biomedical research(oncology) at UCSF with a side passion for all things computer science. I am spending my free time learning programming and web development. I also enjoy photography, cooking, playing the guitar, and painting/drawing.
